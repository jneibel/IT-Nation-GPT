================================================================================
ESCALATION PATH
================================================================================

Level 1: Help Desk Technician
Handles:
- Password resets and account unlocks
- Basic troubleshooting (printer issues, software problems)
- How-to questions and user training
- Ticket creation and documentation

Response: Immediate acknowledgment, resolution per your tier's SLA

When to Escalate: If Level 1 cannot resolve within 50% of SLA window

---

Level 2: Senior Engineer
Handles:
- Complex technical issues
- Infrastructure problems (servers, network, firewalls)
- Multi-user issues
- Advanced troubleshooting

Escalation Trigger: Level 1 unable to resolve, or issue requires elevated permissions/expertise

---

Level 3: Lead Engineer / Solutions Architect
Handles:
- Critical system outages
- Major infrastructure failures
- Architectural issues and design
- Escalated complex problems

Escalation Trigger: 
- Business-critical issues affecting entire organization
- Level 2 unable to resolve
- Issues requiring architectural decisions

---

Level 4: Account Manager / Virtual CIO
Handles:
- Service concerns and satisfaction issues
- Strategic technology guidance
- Billing and contract questions
- Project planning and scoping

Contact When:
- You have concerns about service quality
- Strategic technology planning needed
- Questions about your service agreement
- Project discussion needed
